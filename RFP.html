Study Rooms Booker for University

Study rooms are available in several buildings across the Green Meadows University(GMU) campus for project work, group study and exam study. They are particularly useful for students studying for midterm or final exams and end up in high demand several times during a semester. This demand forces some students to spend a lot of time inefficiently searching for a room instead of studying. It also means that some students get forced to leave rooms because they were unaware that they had been booked. These issues stem from the ambiguity of the current booking system for study rooms. Each department has its own distinct web page and the mobile version of each page is very unintuitive. There are many rooms on campus that could be used by students that cannot be found due to this ambiguity.
